About Kasama FMA

Explore the roots of Filipino martial arts and discover how Kasama FMA blends tradition, modern training, and community to build strength, discipline, and cultural pride.

What is Kali?

Kali is the Filipino martial art of weapon-based fighting, focusing on sticks, blades, and empty-hand techniques. It teaches flow, timing, body mechanics, and awareness so students can move with confidence in both combat and self-defense.

Training in Kali builds coordination, reflexes, and mental clarity — while honoring the Filipino warrior traditions that shaped the art.

What is Kasama FMA?

Kasama FMA is a Filipino martial arts academy built around partnership, respect, and shared growth. “Kasama” means companion or partner, and our training emphasizes working together to preserve tradition while developing real-world skills.

We welcome adults, youth, beginners, and experienced students alike — offering weapon training, empty-hand defense, and a supportive environment for every martial artist.
