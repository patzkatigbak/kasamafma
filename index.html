What is Kasama FMA?

"Kasama" means companion or partner. In our academy, we train together to preserve and sharpen the fluid, deadly, and beautiful art of Filipino weaponry and empty-hand combat. From stick fighting (Arnis/Eskrima/Kali) to tactical self-defense, we carry history forward.
